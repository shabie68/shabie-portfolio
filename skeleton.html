How to improve your confidence

Here are some ways to improve self confidence. I am using these for 4 months and found them very fruitful.

Whenver I want to talk with people, shyness popup in my mind. At the same time I start to questioning my mind like:

  • What is wrong is socializing with people
  • They are not going to hurt me in any way
After 2-3 seconds my mind has no other options than to remove negative feelings. I am amazed at this method. This method is known as Plausible Reasoning
